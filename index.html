VISION

To be a leading, innovative financial cooperative in Malawi.

MISSION

To promote socio-economic stability through savings and credit services to its members for self reliance and community development.

HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER

Be a payroll employee.

Employer consent to join UNC SACCO.

Pay membership fees (K1000 entry + K1000 annual).

Fill out the membership form.

Contribute a minimum of MWK 10,000 monthly.

Membership Withdrawal

Member must not have any outstanding loans.

Give 90 days’ notice.

Withdrawal fees depend on years stayed with SACCO:

  • 1 year - 3% of total shares contributed.
  • 2-3 years - 2% of total shares contributed.
  • 3+ years - 1% of total shares contributed.

All member’s savings will be paid out, minus non-redeemable shares of MWK 20,000.

Bring a Member

Receive a SACCO chitenje for every member you bring.

Bring 6 members at once and receive a SACCO golf shirt.

Receive MWK 20,000 if you bring the whole company or organization. The amount will be deposited into your share account.
